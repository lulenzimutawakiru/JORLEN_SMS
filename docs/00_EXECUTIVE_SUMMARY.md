## Overview

The **Universal Uganda School Management System (U-USMS)** is a comprehensive, cloud-native, multi-tenant SaaS platform designed to serve as the national digital infrastructure for education management across Uganda.

### Key Capabilities

- **Multi-Level School Support:** Nursery to University
- **National Scale:** 10,000+ institutions, 5-8M students
- **Financial Integration:** SchoolPay payment gateway
- **Compliant Payroll:** URA PAYE, NSSF, LST compliance
- **AI-Powered:** Predictive analytics, risk detection, intelligent recommendations
- **Government-Grade Security:** Zero-trust architecture, Data Protection Act 2019 compliant
- **Hybrid Deployment:** Private, Government, PPP models

## System Scope

### Educational Institutions Supported

1. **Nursery & Kindergarten**
   - Child tracking and growth monitoring
   - Non-exam term evaluations
   - Parent communication

2. **Primary Schools**
   - PLE grading structure
   - Competency-based curriculum
   - Continuous assessment

3. **Secondary Schools**
   - UCE & UACE grading
   - Subject-based teacher allocation
   - Stream/class automation

4. **Vocational & Technical Institutions**
   - Modular course structure
   - Practical assessments
   - Certification and internship tracking

5. **Universities & Tertiary**
   - Credit-hour system
   - GPA calculation
   - Semester billing
   - Academic transcripts
