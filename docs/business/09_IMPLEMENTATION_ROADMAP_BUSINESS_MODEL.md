# Implementation Roadmap & Business Model - U-USMS

## Executive Summary

U-USMS is positioned to become Uganda's national education management platform, serving 10,000+ institutions and 5-8M students with projected annual revenue of UGX 7B+ by Year 5.

---

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. SaaS Subscriptions (Primary Revenue)

**Tiered Pricing:**

| Tier | School Size | Price/Student/Year (UGX) | Features |
|------|------------|------------------------|----------|
| **Starter** | <200 students | 6,000 | Basic features, community support |
| **Professional** | 200-1,000 | 4,500 | All features, email support |
| **Enterprise** | 1,000-5,000 | 3,500 | Premium features, priority support |
| **Network** | 5,000+ | 3,000 | Custom features, dedicated account manager |

**Government Pricing:**
- Negotiated bulk licensing
- Volume discounts (30-50% off commercial rates)
- Multi-year contracts

#### 2. Value-Added Services

**SMS Bundles:**
- Pay-as-you-go: UGX 50/SMS
- Monthly package: 1,000 SMS @ UGX 40,000 (UGX 40/SMS)
- Enterprise: 10,000 SMS @ UGX 350,000 (UGX 35/SMS)

**Advanced AI Features:**
- Performance prediction: UGX 50,000/school/month
- Dropout risk detection: UGX 40,000/school/month
- AI chatbot: UGX 60,000/school/month

**Custom Integrations:**
- One-time setup: UGX 5,000,000 - 20,000,000
- Ongoing support: 20% of setup cost annually

**Training & Consulting:**
- On-site training: UGX 2,000,000/day
- Remote training: UGX 500,000/session
- Custom consulting: UGX 300,000/hour

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Target Segments

**Primary (Year 1):**
1. Private primary schools in urban areas (Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja)
2. Medium-sized secondary schools (500-1500 students)

**Secondary (Year 2):**
3. Government-aided schools
4. Church-founded schools
5. School networks (e.g., Aga Khan schools)

**Tertiary (Year 3+):**
6. Vocational institutions
7. Universities
8. International schools

### Marketing Channels

**Digital:**
- Website with live demo
- Social media (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- Google Ads (education keywords)
